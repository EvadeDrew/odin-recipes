Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for lasagna

A lot of cultures enjoy lasagna so it would be a good variety to learn

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg
    •

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Layer the lasagna correctly
  4. Cover the foil and bake
  5. Let lasagna sit before serving
