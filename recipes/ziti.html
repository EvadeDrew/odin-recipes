Baked Ziti

Description

Baked Ziti is a very famous and tasty italian dish

If you like pasta, you'll love baked ziti

Ingredients

  • Kosher Salt
  • Italian sausage
  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese (Ricotta & Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Preheat oven at 350 F
  2. Cook the pasta
  3. Brown the meat
  4. Make the sauce
  5. Assemble the casserole
  6. Bake !
